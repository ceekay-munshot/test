## Key Governance Highlights

**STRENGTHS:**
- Board: >50% independent directors; non-executive chairman; strong director credentials
- Audit: Big 4 auditor (SRBC/EY); clean audit opinion for FY25; mandatory rotation followed
- Promoter: Stable 55% stake; 0.01% pledge; 100+ year business vintage; no family conflicts
- Financials: Negative working capital cycle (-32 days); low leverage (D/E 0.26); EBITDA margins 20%+
- Regulatory: No ED/SEBI cases; minor tax demands only; clean compliance record
- Transparency: 39 analyst coverage; detailed quarterly calls; full SEBI LODR compliance

**WATCH POINTS:**
- Dividend inconsistency: FY24 payout cut 43% to Rs 80/share (though FY25 rebounded strongly to Rs 210)
- Succession planning: No named successor to Rajiv Bajaj despite his July 2024 public comments about needing a "younger MD"
- FII trends: FII holding declining (-1.87% in Q1 FY25); monitor for continued outflows
- Subsidiary audit: Overseas subsidiary auditors not confirmed as Big 4 across all entities
- RPTs: Material related-party transactions with Bajaj Group entities; arm's length pricing should be monitored
