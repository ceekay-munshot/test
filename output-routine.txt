### Key Governance Highlights (Bajaj Auto)

**STRENGTHS:**
- Board composition: 54.5% independent directors; clear separation of Chairman (Niraj Bajaj) and MD&CEO (Rajiv Bajaj)
- Big 4 auditor (SRBC/EY); clean, unqualified audit opinion for FY2024-25
- Zero debt; net cash company with INR ~17,000 crore cash/investments as of FY2025
- Consistent dividend payer (INR 210/share in FY25) + INR 4,000 crore buyback in FY2024
- Nil promoter pledge; stable promoter stake ~55% over 8+ quarters
- Exceptional promoter vintage (Bajaj Group since 1926; 100-year legacy)
- Transformative CEO Rajiv Bajaj with 30 years at Bajaj Auto; re-appointed till Mar 2030
- Record financials: Revenue INR 50,000 crore, EBITDA INR 10,000 crore, PAT INR 8,000+ crore (FY25)
- CFO/EBITDA ~73%; high earnings quality
- No material regulatory (SEBI/ED/CBI) proceedings
- Full SEBI LODR compliance; high analyst coverage (30+ analysts)

**AREAS TO MONITOR:**
- Working capital cycle expansion (from ~10 days to ~68 days) — warrants tracking in FY26
- Employee attrition rate not explicitly disclosed; headcount declined 12.7% in FY24
- Bajaj Group conglomerate structure (multiple listed entities) — potential governance complexity
- EV subsidiary (Bajaj Auto Technology Ltd) investment phase — capex returns to be watched
